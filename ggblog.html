Trying out Mobirise

By GuiGhost posted April 17, 2017

"Make your own website in a few clicks", that was the promise. Well I decided to try it out and this page is a product of the tool. Mobrise comes with some built in templates, a few more you can download, and then the more complex "blocks" can be purchased individually or as a package. You might want to check it out if you are interested in a no coding approach to building a mobile friendly simple website for small to medium businesses. This relies mostly on Bootstrap templates and an engine that installs as a a local application. The setup was easy and quick. After playing with a few of the options, this tool may warrant more investigation.

    As I look into it further, I will update this article with more findings on this tool that can be used as a quick start to simple websites. Stay tuned for more or check it our yourself at mobirise.com!
