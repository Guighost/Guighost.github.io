Trying out Mobrise

By GuiGhost posted April 17, 2017

Make your own website in a few clicks, that was the promise. Well I decided to try it out and this page is a product of the tool. Mobrise comes with some built in templates, a few more you can download, and then the more complex "blocks" can be purchased individually or as a package. You might want to check it out if you are interested in a no coding approach to building a mobile friendly simple website for small to medium businesses. This relys mostly on bootstrap templates and an engine that installs as a a local application.
